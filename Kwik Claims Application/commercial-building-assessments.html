Commercial Building Assessments

Millions being recovered. When commercial properties have hidden or long‑running issues, a thorough building assessment can uncover scope, document conditions, and identify what insurance may cover vs. out‑of‑pocket retail costs. We tailor the inspection checklist and report to your building type and age, and we run targeted tests to diagnose issues. Commercial inspections are $4,500 — the fee is waived if one of our contracting partners is selected for the scope of work (insurance‑covered or retail).

What we do

We perform a full building inspection to diagnose issues, document conditions, and deliver a report you can act on — for restoration, renovation, conversion, or operations planning.

  • Tailored checklist — We research the building type, age, and common failure points and build a custom inspection checklist.
  • On-site inspection + documentation — Systematic walkthrough, photos, measurements, and condition notes.
  • Actionable report — Prioritized repairs, recommended next steps, and condition summary.
  • Insurance coverage insight — General guidance on what insurance may cover (coverage is policy‑specific).
  • Out‑of‑pocket cost guidance — Approximate retail cost ranges to fix items outside coverage.

Pricing
