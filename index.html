FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you take a percentage of the insurance money?+
No. We do not take any percentage of claim proceeds. You pay a flat monthly subscription for the unlimited‑claims option (or a separate fee for other services).
What do you need from my team to start?+
Photos, basic job info, and an intro to the homeowner/property contact. We tell you exactly what documentation to capture so the file is complete.
Do you only work residential claims?+
No. We support residential and commercial workflows depending on the service (including commercial building assessments).
Can you review previously denied claims?+
Yes. Members can request a review of prior denied/underpaid claims to see if there’s a path to re‑open or supplement based on the documentation.
Can you build software for my company?+
Yes. We can build a custom web app that mirrors the system we use — tailored to your company, pricing, and workflow.
